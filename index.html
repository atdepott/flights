About

Flight data is from the Bureau of Transportation Statistics. Available here.

Travel minutes per route are calculated based on average travel time between origin and destination during 2013. Routes with less than 100 flights/year were discarded. The fastest possible routes between non-adjacent cities were calculated assuming a 60-minute layover in each intermediate location.
